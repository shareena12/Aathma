ABOUT US

At AATHMA we believe that mental well-being is just as important as physical health. Our mission is to provide accessible, compassionate, and science-backed support for individuals seeking to improve their mental health. Designed by experts in psychology, therapy, and mindfulness, AATHMA offers personalized tools to help you manage stress, anxiety, depression, and other challenges. Whether you're looking for guided meditation, cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) techniques, journaling prompts, or access to licensed professionals, our app is here to support you every step of the way. We are committed to breaking the stigma around mental health and empowering individuals to take charge of their well-being in a safe, inclusive, and judgment-free space. Join our community and start your journey toward a healthier mind today.
